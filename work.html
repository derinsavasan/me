Selected things I have built. All source code on Git.

Shapes of Stories — 2026
An interactive visualization that maps the emotional arcs of 1,627 Hollywood screenplays (1980–2025) onto Kurt Vonnegut’s six story shapes, using sentiment analysis and ML.
Python, D3, React, TypeScript

Florida Man — 2025
A static scrollytelling analysis of 39,344 “Florida Man” headlines to investigate what makes the nature of their crimes so unique; sort by theme, season, and county.
Python, D3, Scrollama, BeautifulSoup, Pushshift

Remember Me? — 2025
A connection game made with the Smithsonian’s National Portrait Gallery, where matching its Revolution-era portraits reveals who history left out.
JavaScript, Hugging Face, HTML, CSS

Sentimental Value — 2025
A professional collaboration with Netflix that turns their 2025 viewership data into a sentiment matrix; plots every title by how deeply and widely it was watched.
JavaScript, D3, HTML, CSS

Choke — 2025
An interactive study of the mandatory choking posters from every NYC restaurant I have been to, compared against the official design.
JavaScript, D3, Leaflet, HTML, CSS
